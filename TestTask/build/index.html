Join us in St. Petersburg on November 22, 2014 for the EPAM Engineering Conference. Network and brainstorm with the smartest engineers in the world, discovering new ways to solve the latest challenges in the industry. Share your interest and passion with EPAMers like you.

The event

Every year, EPAM’s Software Engineering Conference (SEC) gives EPAMers a chance to exchange knowledge and solutions while learning the latest industry trends. It’s one of the key ways our company:

Shares information & expertise

Builds excitement & pride in the work we've done

Informs the future of the digital landscape

The Speakers

Learn about EPAM’s innovative work and solutions around the globe from expert EPAMers and industry thought leaders. Speakers from within EPAM are invited to participate in SEC, and then invited to represent the company at external conferences as well.

This combination of internal and external experts creates talks, workshops, panel discussions and more to reveal the future of the digital landscape.
